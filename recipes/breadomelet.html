Bread Omelet

Good for health

Taking bread omelet for break fast gives you dizzy feeling after taking a juice it will act as a sleeping dose so take rest for a while and grind.

ingredients

  • two eggs
  • two breads
  • two spoons butter
  • one tomato
  • one onion

Steps

  1. stir two eggs well
  2. chop tomato and onion
  3. On the stove
  4. take a pan and place over the stove
  5. wait for two minutes
  6. spread two spoons of butter over the preheated pan
  7. spread egg through out the pan
  8. place two breads over the egg
  9. place chopped tomato and onion over one side of the bread
  10. flip one bread over veg side
  11. eat and rest

noodles

Good for health

Taking Noodles for break fast gives you dizzy feeling after taking a juice it will act as a sleeping dose so take rest for a while and grind.

ingredients

  • two eggs
  • Noodles
  • two spoons butter
  • one tomato
  • one onion

Steps

  1. stir two eggs well
  2. chop tomato and onion
  3. On the stove
  4. take a pan and place over the stove
  5. wait for two minutes
  6. spread two spoons of butter over the preheated pan
  7. spread egg through out the pan and take separately
  8. On the stove
  9. take a pan and place over the stove
  10. add water two cups and masala
  11. put noodles and add chopped tomato and onion
  12. wait for two minutes
  13. eat and rest
